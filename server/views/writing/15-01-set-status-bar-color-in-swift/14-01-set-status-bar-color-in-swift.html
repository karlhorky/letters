--- layout: post title: Set Status Bar Color in Swift description: Where you put it tags: [swift, ios] --- Within your ```AppDelegate.swift```, in ```didFinishLaunchingWithOptions```: ```UINavigationbar.appearance().barTintColor = UIColor.greenColor()``` Optionally, if you need a specific color, how you do it with RGB: ```UINavigationBar.appearance().barTintColor = UIColor(red: 1.0, green: 1.0, blue: 1.0, alpha: 1.0)```
